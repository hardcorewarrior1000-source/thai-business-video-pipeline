## SCRIPT RULES (OpenCode follows these automatically)

LENGTH:
- 1500-2200 words for 10-13 minute video
- Target: 150 words per minute × minutes = word count
- Thai is more compact than English — err on the shorter side

FORMAT:
- Pure narration only — no headers, bullets, visual cues, stage directions
- Thai language, 2nd person ("คุณ")
- English only for brand/company names (e.g., "CP", "Central", "Grab")
- Save as: output/[topic-slug]/script.txt

VOICE:
- Smart friend who knows business gossip — calm but energized
- Never "เรา" or "ผม/ฉัน" — always "คุณ"
- Tone: revealing, insider-ish, slightly amazed at the twists
- "Sanuk" first — engaging and fun, never dry or academic

RHYTHM:
- Short sentences. Short sentences. One longer sentence that adds depth. Short sentences. Question?
- Use "..." for short pauses
- Blank line = longer pause / new section
- Question every 4-6 sentences
- Place a re-engagement hook at 25-35% of script (retention valley rescue)

CONTENT REQUIREMENTS:
- Hook in first 4 lines — show the SHOCKING RESULT first (Immediate Value hook, 78% retention)
- Reference at least 3 real data points naturally (revenue, market share, growth %, etc.)
- Every business term gets plain explanation immediately
- Curiosity Loop: each section ends with a question that pulls into the next
- End with sentence that echoes the opening, reframed with the lesson learned

NARRATIVE ARC (Curiosity Loop):
1. **Hook** — Shocking result or counterintuitive fact (0-5s pattern interrupt)
2. **Setup** — Why this matters to YOU (5-15s payoff promise)
3. **The Story** — What happened, told as a narrative with tension
4. **Counterintuitive Twist** — The thing nobody expected
5. **The Lesson** — What you can apply from this
6. **Echo** — Circle back to opening, now reframed

## OUTPUT FORMAT

The file should contain ONLY the narration text. Example:

---

ลองจินตนาการว่าคุณกำลังยืนอยู่หน้าร้านสะดวกซื้อ...

คุณเห็นโลโก้สีเขียว-ส้มที่คุณเห็นมาตั้งแต่เด็ก

แต่คุณเคยสงสัยไหม... ว่าทำไมร้านนี้ถึงมีอยู่ทุกมุมถนนในประเทศไทย?

คำตอบอาจจะทำให้คุณตกใจ...

เพราะมันไม่ใช่แค่ร้านขายของ มันคือระบบที่ออกแบบมาเพื่อให้คุณกลับมาทุกวัน

---

## WHAT TO AVOID

- No "สวัสดีครับ/ค่ะ" opening — jump straight into hook
- No "วันนี้เราจะมา..." — overused
- No bullet points or numbered lists in narration
- No stage directions like [pause] or [visual: ...]
- No English words unless they're brand/company names
- No "คุณผู้ชม" — just "คุณ"
- No dry financial reporting — this is storytelling, not a textbook
- No political commentary or Section 112 references
