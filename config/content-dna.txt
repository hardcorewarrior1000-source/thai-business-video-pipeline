=== CONTENT DNA — วิเคราะห์ธุรกิจไทย (Thai Business Case Studies) ===

NICHE:
- Thai business case studies: why companies succeed, fail, or disrupt
- Target audience: Thai viewers 18-40 interested in business, entrepreneurship, and money
- Language: Thai narration, English for brand/company names only
- Format: Story-driven analysis, not dry financial reporting

VOICE & NARRATION STYLE:
- Calm but energized, like a smart friend who knows business gossip
- 2nd person ("คุณ") — never "เรา" or "ผม/ฉัน"
- Tone: revealing, insider-ish, slightly amazed at the twists
- "Sanuk" first — if it's not fun to listen to, rewrite it
- Speak Thai (3.2x engagement vs English-heavy content)

HOOK FORMULA (3-Phase):
- 0-5s: Pattern interrupt — shocking result or counterintuitive fact
- 5-15s: Payoff promise — "ในคลิปนี้คุณจะรู้ว่า..."
- 15-30s: Commitment hook — curiosity loop that makes leaving painful
- Best hook type: "Immediate Value" (78% retention) — show the result FIRST, then explain how

SCRIPT RHYTHM:
- Short sentences. Short sentences. One longer sentence that adds depth. Short sentences. Question?
- Question every 4-6 sentences to maintain engagement
- Curiosity Loop narrative = flattest retention curves (1.2% drop/min vs 2.8% avg)
- Place re-engagement hook at 25-35% of video (retention valley)

NARRATIVE ARC (Curiosity Loop):
Hook (shocking result) → Setup (why this matters) → The Story (what happened) → Counterintuitive Twist → The Lesson (what you can learn) → Echo of opening

CONTENT RULES:
- Every business term gets plain-language explanation immediately
- Reference at least 3 real data points/sources per video
- Numbers/stats make claims credible — use them
- Real Thai companies, real data — no made-up examples
- End by connecting the business lesson to something the viewer experiences daily
- No jargon without translation
- Respect Section 112 — avoid political commentary

VIRAL TOPIC ANGLES (Thai Business):
1. "ทำไม [Thai company] ถึง [surprising outcome]?" — Case study format
2. "เบื้องหลังความสำเร็จ/ความล้มเหลวของ [brand]" — Behind-the-scenes reveal
3. "[Company A] vs [Company B] ใครชนะ?" — Competitive analysis
4. "สิ่งที่ [brand] ไม่อยากให้คุณรู้" — Insider secrets
5. "ธุรกิจที่คุณไม่เคยรู้ว่าทำเงินได้เท่านี้" — Hidden money machines
6. "จาก [humble beginning] สู่ [empire]" — Origin story arc
7. "ความผิดพลาดครั้งใหญ่ที่สุดของ [company]" — Failure analysis

RESEARCH SOURCES TO REFERENCE:
- SET (Stock Exchange of Thailand) filings and annual reports
- Department of Business Development (DBD) data
- BrandInside, The Standard, thumbsupTH articles
- Nielsen, Kantar market research for Thailand
- SEC Thailand for financial disclosures
- Google Trends Thailand for search interest data
- SimilarWeb for traffic/engagement data
- Thai startup ecosystem: Techsauce, Disrupt Technology Venture

THUMBNAIL STYLE:
- High contrast, 3-5 words MAX in Thai
- Before/after, winner/loser, or shocking number
- Works at mobile size (most Thai viewers are mobile)
- Brand logos recognizable even small
